SEO

What is seo

SEO - a set of measures to promote the site in search engines and the conclusion of the site's position in the "top" - that the site on the query of interest to us was on the first page of the search engine (and there, as you know, only 10 links are placed).

This is necessary to increase the attendance of the site, since most of the visitors to the site come from search engines. Therefore, the higher our site in the issuance of the search engine - the more visitors it has.

Popular Search Engines

The most popular search engines on our territory are Google and Yandex and it is for their attention to their site and is a constant struggle between webmasters. The position of the site in the issuance of the search engine depends on the relevance of the user's request to the content of the site and from special indexes. For Google it's PR (Page Rank), and for Yandex - TIC (thematic citation index). The more these indicators, the higher the position of the site compared to competitors.
